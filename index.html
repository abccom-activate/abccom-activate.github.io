What is the activation code and where you can find it?

The activation code is a unique code provided by the ABC network to activate their services on various devices, such as: Smart TVs, Streaming devices (e.g., Roku, Amazon Fire TV), Mobile apps (e.g., iOS, Android), Gaming consoles (e.g., Xbox, PlayStation).

You can find the activation code in the following ways:

  • On your TV screen: When you launch the ABC app on your device, an activation code will be displayed on the screen.
  • In the ABC app: Open the ABC app on your device, go to the settings or account section, and look for the activation code.
  • On the ABC website: Visit the ABC website ([link unavailable]), sign in to your account, and look for the activation code in your account settings.
  • In an email from ABC: If you've subscribed to ABC services, you might receive an email with the activation code.

How to Install and use the ABC Channel on Fire TV?

  • Start by going to the Amazon App Store to search for the “ABC” application by using the search bar.
  • Now, on the ABC app’s detailed page, install the ABC app.
  • In the next step, open the ABC app on your Fire TV, just to receive the activation code.
  • Next, launch a browser and visit the abc.com/activate link on your computer or smartphone.
  • Right on the activation tab, insert the code and then press the Continue button.
  • In the next step, choose the TV network that you have been enrolled in, and by using your login details complete the login process.
  • Finally, watch ABC on your Amazon Fire TV.

How to activate your account at abc.com/activate?

  • Go to abc.com/activate: Open a web browser and navigate to the ABC activation page.
  • Enter the activation code: Type in the activation code displayed on your device (TV, streaming device, or mobile app).
  • Select your device: Choose the device you're using to activate ABC (e.g., Smart TV, Roku, Apple TV, etc.).
  • Sign in to your ABC account: Enter your ABC account email and password. If you don't have an account, create a new one.
  • Verify your account: ABC may send a verification email to your email address. Click the link in the email to verify your account.
  • Complete the activation process: Follow any additional prompts to complete the activation process.
  • Start watching ABC: Once activated, you can access ABC content on your device.
